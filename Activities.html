Approaching Art III

In the course of the 20th century, new technologies impacted on artistic creations in many ways, due to their direct incorporation in the field of art-as in the case of film and photography-or the repercussions they brought.

PROGRAM

November 5: Photomontage as derived from Cubist collage and the Surrealist symbolic object.
